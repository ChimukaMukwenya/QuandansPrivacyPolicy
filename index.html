Privacy Policy

Welcome to Quandans AI Study Assistant. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our AI-powered study assistance application.

Last Updated: June 2025

By using Quandans AI Study Assistant, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We respect your privacy and are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of any personal information you provide to us.

Our AI Study Assistant is designed to help students with their academic work while maintaining the highest standards of data privacy and security. We only collect information that is necessary to provide you with the best possible study assistance experience.

Data Collection and Usage

Information We Collect:

  • Study Content: Questions, documents, and academic materials you submit for AI assistance
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and session duration
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support
  • Settings and Preferences: Your customized AI response styles, themes, and download paths
  • Error Logs: Technical information to help us improve app performance and fix issues

How We Use Your Information:

  • Provide personalized AI study assistance and academic support
  • Improve our AI algorithms and enhance user experience
  • Troubleshoot technical issues and provide customer support
  • Analyze usage patterns to develop new features and improvements
  • Ensure the security and proper functioning of our services

What We Don't Do: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal study data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your academic content remains private and is not used to train AI models that could be accessed by other users.

Data Security and Storage

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Local Storage: Your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using secure methods
  • Data Minimization: We only collect and retain data that is necessary for app functionality
  • Regular Security Audits: Our systems undergo regular security assessments and updates
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access user data

Data Retention: Study session data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers. User preferences and settings are stored locally on your device. We retain anonymized usage analytics for up to 12 months to improve our services.

Data Deletion: You can delete your local data at any time by clearing the app's data through your device settings. Upon uninstalling the app, all locally stored data is automatically removed.
